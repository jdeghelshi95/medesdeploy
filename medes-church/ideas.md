# MEDES Church — Design Ideas

## Idea 1: Sacred Brutalism
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Sacred Brutalism — raw, powerful, unapologetically bold.

**Core Principles:**
- Heavy typographic contrast: ultra-bold display text against fine-weight captions
- Dark charcoal/near-black backgrounds with fiery orange-red accents
- Asymmetric layouts with intentional tension and visual weight
- Honest materials: no decorative fluff, every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#111111) base evokes solemnity and reverence. Flame orange (#E8450A) and ember red (#C0392B) represent the Holy Spirit's fire. Warm off-white (#F5F0E8) for readable body text. The palette communicates urgency and spiritual intensity.

**Layout Paradigm:** Off-center hero with large typographic lockup pushed left. Video section uses a dominant player on the left with Bible reader as a vertical sidebar on the right — like a study desk. Services grid uses oversized numbers as visual anchors.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick horizontal rule dividers in flame orange
- Large numerals as section markers (01, 02, 03)
- Subtle grain texture overlay on hero backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal rather than decorate — text slides, borders extend, colors shift. Nothing bounces or floats; everything slides with authority.

**Animation:** Entrance animations use translate-Y with opacity fades (no scale). Staggered reveals for list items. No looping animations except the hero flame particle effect.

**Typography System:** "Bebas Neue" for display headings (all-caps, ultra-condensed), "Lora" for body text (serif warmth), "JetBrains Mono" for verse references.
</text>

## Idea 2: Luminous Sanctuary
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Sacred — clean, luminous, welcoming.

**Core Principles:**
- Deep navy/midnight backgrounds with warm gold and amber accents
- Editorial layout inspired by modern church magazines
- Generous whitespace that creates breathing room and contemplation
- Layered depth through glassmorphism cards and soft glows

**Color Philosophy:** Midnight navy (#0D1B2A) as the primary background evokes the night sky and spiritual depth. Gold (#D4A843) represents divine light and glory. Warm white (#FEFCF8) for text. Subtle teal (#2A6B7C) as a secondary accent for interactive elements.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with centered text and a cinematic video background. Below hero, a two-column layout: video player left (60%) + Bible reader right (40%) with a glowing separator. Services section uses horizontal scrolling cards.

**Signature Elements:**
- Soft radial glow behind key headings (gold halo effect)
- Glassmorphism cards with frosted blur backgrounds
- Thin gold border accents on section dividers

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, reverent transitions. Cards lift gently on hover. Bible reader has a warm parchment-like scroll feel. Everything feels like entering a quiet, well-lit sanctuary.

**Animation:** Fade-in with subtle upward drift on scroll. Gold shimmer effect on CTA buttons. Bible verse transitions with a gentle cross-fade.

**Typography System:** "Playfair Display" for headings (elegant serif with high contrast), "Source Sans 3" for body (clean, readable), "EB Garamond" for Bible verses (classic and reverent).
</text>

## Idea 3: Urban Revival (CHOSEN)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Urban Revival — street-level energy meets spiritual depth.

**Core Principles:**
- Dark charcoal base with vivid flame gradient (orange → red) as the brand signature
- Bold, asymmetric editorial layouts with strong visual hierarchy
- Photography-forward design with cinematic overlays
- Kinetic energy in transitions that mirrors the church's passionate worship style

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0F0F0F) as the canvas. The MEDES flame gradient (from #FF6B35 through #E8450A to #C0392B) is the brand's heartbeat — used for CTAs, accents, and the logo. Warm white (#F8F4EF) for primary text. Muted stone (#8A8580) for secondary text. This palette mirrors the existing brand while elevating it dramatically.

**Layout Paradigm:** Sticky nav with frosted glass effect. Hero uses a full-bleed dark image with the church name in a large, left-aligned display font. The video + Bible reader section uses a 55/45 split with the video player dominant and the Bible reader as an elegant companion panel. No centered layouts.

**Signature Elements:**
- Flame gradient as a recurring motif (borders, buttons, logo glow)
- Diagonal section dividers creating visual momentum
- Cinematic video thumbnails with hover-reveal play buttons

**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything responds with warmth and energy. Hover states use the flame gradient. The Bible reader has chapter/verse navigation that feels like flipping through a physical book. YouTube cards reveal metadata on hover with a smooth lift.

**Animation:** Scroll-triggered fade-ups with slight horizontal drift. Nav items underline with a flame gradient on hover. Video cards scale subtly on hover (1.02). Bible verse changes cross-fade smoothly.

**Typography System:** "Oswald" for display headings (bold, condensed, powerful), "Nunito Sans" for body text (friendly, readable), "Crimson Pro" for Bible verses (literary, reverent).
</text>

## Selected Design: Urban Revival

The **Urban Revival** approach best matches MEDES Church's existing brand identity (flame logo, dark backgrounds, passionate community) while dramatically modernizing the experience. The flame gradient ties directly to the existing logo, the asymmetric layouts break from the generic Wix template feel, and the photography-forward approach showcases the church's vibrant worship services.
